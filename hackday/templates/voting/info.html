How To Vote

On the site

  1. Click here to vote on the site.

By Text or Email

Text: YOUR NUMBER HERE
Email: YOUR EMAIL HERE

  1. Make sure the email or phone number you are sending from has been added to your account profile.
  2. Choose the number next to a category.
  3. Choose the number next to a team.
  4. Compose a message (email or SMS) with those two numbers in the format category#-team#.
  5. Send your message.
  6. You can submit votes for multiple categories in a single message. Just toss a space between votes. For example, to submit a vote for team #3 in category #7 and team #10 in category #5, your message would look like this: 7-3 5-10
